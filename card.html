Cat

There's over 500 millions pet cats around the world and there's around 40 breeds.

  • Scientific name: Felis catus
  • Average lifespan: 16 years
  • Favorite food: Meat
  • Cat's family: Felidae

To some people, cats are the perfect pet, elegant, calming companions, capable of dealing with their own exercise and cleaning. To others, they are frustratingly independent, cool and aloof.
